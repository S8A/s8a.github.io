Solow-Swan growth model

Published date: 27 May 2019

A Calculus 4 assignment about the application of differential equations, made in GeoGebra. In this interactive document I explored the Solow-Swan economic growth model, which is a nonlinear system consisting of a single ordinary differential equation that models the evolution of the per capita stock of capital.
