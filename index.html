Samuel Ochoa

I’m an information systems engineer (graduated in 2023) and self-taught software developer from Venezuela. I’ve worked as a software developer both as an employee and as a freelancer, besides coding for personal projects. Check out the about page to learn more about my skills, work experience, and education.

Feel free to reach out to me for collaboration opportunities, software development projects, or any other professional inquiries.
